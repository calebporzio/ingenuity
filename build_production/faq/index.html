    • What is the cost?

    Because there is so much flexibility with amount of people, length of game, and location it is very hard to list costs. I would love to give you a personal quote after I know more about your needs and group size.

    How many people play at once?

    The ideal group size would be from 8 to 10 people.

    How large of a group can you handle?

    We can have multiple groups play in succession. It takes us less than 15 minutes to reset the room after a team plays. So we can handle large groups over a period of time.

    What are the time constraints?

    We have made the rooms adaptable to your needs. We can vary the length from 45 minutes to 60 minutes to 75 minutes. If something shorter or longer is requested we will do our best to meet your needs.

    How long does set-up, game reset, and clean up take?

    We can set-up the room in less than 1 hour. Reset, for multiple teams, takes 15 minutes. Clean up takes about 30 minutes. Our team takes care of all the details.

    Is this just for businesses or would you come to my home?

    Although team building for businesses is our main goal, a natural extension of this is to take the escape rooms to homes for families to experience. This can be an exciting adventure for family reunions, parties, or just hanging out with a group of friends.

    What size room is needed?

    We need a room that is big enough for 8 to 10 people to comfortably move around in. A typical conference room is usually perfect. Ideally we would come to your business or home first and make sure we have a good space to use.
